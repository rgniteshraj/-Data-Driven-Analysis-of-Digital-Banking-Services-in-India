# 📊 Data-Driven Analysis of Digital Banking Services in India

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes India's digital banking ecosystem using **Python** and **Power BI**. The objective is to analyze banking infrastructure, digital payment systems, and online transaction trends to generate meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow, including data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, dashboard development, and business intelligence reporting.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Banks generate a large amount of digital banking data through ATMs, POS terminals, debit cards, credit cards, online banking, and UPI transactions. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify banking trends, compare bank performance, and make informed business decisions.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing digital banking data and presenting insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze banking infrastructure across major Indian banks.
- Compare ATM networks and POS terminal deployment.
- Analyze debit card and credit card usage.
- Study online transaction trends.
- Compare different bank categories.
- Build interactive Power BI dashboards.
- Generate meaningful business insights.
- Provide business recommendations.

# 📊 Dashboard 1 – Banking Infrastructure Overview

### KPI Cards

- Total ATMs
- Total POS Terminals
- Total Debit Cards
- Total Credit Cards
- Total Online Transaction Value

### Visualizations

- Top 10 Banks by ATM Network
- Top 10 Banks by POS Terminals
- Debit Cards vs Credit Cards
- Bank Category Distribution

### Key Insights

- State Bank of India has the largest ATM network.
- Axis Bank has the highest POS deployment.
- Debit cards account for over 90% of total cards.
- Private Sector Banks dominate banking infrastructure.

---

# 📈 Dashboard 2 – Digital Banking Analysis & Business Insights

### Visualizations

- Online Transaction Trend
- ATM Network vs POS
- Online Transaction Value by Bank Category
- UPI QR Distribution
- ATM Withdrawal Distribution

### Key Insights

- Online transactions have steadily increased.
- Banks with more ATMs generally have more POS terminals.
- Private Sector Banks lead digital banking adoption.
- ATM withdrawals are concentrated in lower-value ranges.
